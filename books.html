companionship

expected: end of 2026

in japan they call it rent-a-boyfriend. in europe, companions. in tallinn, a man offers exactly that: paid presence for the lonely, the elderly. he is good at being what others need. the job suits him. that is also the problem. companionship is what it costs him.
